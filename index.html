Our Story

Journey to the bygone era.

Dates County will take you back in time to the roots of evolution. When mankind enjoyed nature amidst its original creations. When stones and boulders, streams and soil, hills and hillocks - all made the landscape together. Close to an environment that's like a village - in the nearness of a temple. Get set for a place that has the best of the past, with the best of the future. Where life is simple, pure, and where you can stay healthy and happy - both physically and mentally.
